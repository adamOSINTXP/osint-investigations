# Case: Weekly OSINT Challenge #01 – Vehicle Identification (Taxi)

## Objective
Identify the vehicle identification number (VIN) of a taxi likely used by a suspect fleeing a recovered stolen vehicle, using publicly available information and historical context.

---

## Initial Data
- Scenario: Stolen vehicle recovered in 2009  
- Suspect fled the area using a nearby taxi  
- Image provided:
  - Taken in a later year (not 2009)  
  - Shows two large resort buildings  
  - Palm trees and roadway visible  
- No taxi visible in the original image  

---

## Investigation Process

### Step 1: Landmark Identification
- Analyzed the image for recognizable structures  
- Identified two distinct resort buildings  
- Performed reverse image search to confirm identity  

Buildings identified as:
- **Encore Resort**
- **Wynn Resort** (Las Vegas)

---

### Step 2: Geolocation
- Used identified landmarks to determine location near the Las Vegas Strip  
- Analyzed road positioning relative to buildings  
- Determined the image perspective aligned with **Fashion Show Drive**

---

### Step 3: Temporal Analysis (Key Step)
- Recognized the provided image was taken in a later year (approx. 2017)  
- Needed to reconstruct the same viewpoint in **2009**  

---

### Step 4: Historical Scene Reconstruction
- Used Google Maps and Street View  
- Navigated to Fashion Show Drive  
- Utilized historical imagery to filter for **2009 timeframe**  
- Matched:
  - building angles  
  - road layout  
  - environmental features  

Successfully recreated the original scene as it would have appeared in 2009

---

### Step 5: Vehicle Identification
- Located a taxi present in the reconstructed 2009 imagery  
- Captured a screenshot of the vehicle for analysis  

---

### Step 6: Image Enhancement
- Used **Forensically** to improve clarity of the vehicle image  
- Adjusted contrast and sharpness to reveal obscured details  

---

### Step 7: VIN Extraction
- Analyzed the enhanced image  
- Identified visible number sequence corresponding to the vehicle’s VIN  
- Verified the extracted number matched expected challenge format  

---

## Findings
- Original image location identified as Wynn/Encore area in Las Vegas  
- Perspective matched to Fashion Show Drive  
- Scene successfully reconstructed using 2009 historical imagery  
- Taxi identified within reconstructed scene  
- Image enhancement revealed previously unreadable identifying information  

---

## Conclusion
By combining landmark identification, geolocation, temporal analysis, and historical imagery reconstruction, the scene was recreated in its 2009 state. This allowed for identification and analysis of a taxi present at that time, ultimately leading to successful extraction of the vehicle’s identification number (VIN).

---

## Skills Demonstrated
- OSINT methodology  
- Geolocation analysis  
- Temporal analysis (multi-year comparison)  
- Historical imagery reconstruction  
- Image forensics (Forensically)  
- Analytical reasoning  

---

## Lessons Learned
- Time context is critical in investigations involving historical events  
- Reconstructing past environments can reveal data not visible in current imagery  
- Combining geolocation with temporal filtering significantly improves investigative accuracy  
- Image enhancement tools are essential for extracting hidden or degraded information  

---

## Final Determination
VIN: 3822
