# Case: Weekly OSINT Challenge #02 – Device Identification (Laptop)

## Objective
Determine the most likely brand (make) of a laptop confiscated from a suspect during an arrest, using open-source intelligence techniques.

---

## Initial Data
- Scenario: Suspect apprehended while fleeing in a taxi  
- Authorities confiscated several items, including a laptop  
- Task: Identify the brand (MAKE) of the laptop  
- Suspect identified as: **Ross William Ulbricht**

---

## Investigation Process

### Step 1: Identity Verification
- Identified the suspect as **Ross William Ulbricht**  
- Conducted research to confirm whether the individual was real or fictional  

Determined:
- Ross Ulbricht is a real individual  
- Known as the creator and operator of the Silk Road darknet marketplace  
- Arrested in 2013 by U.S. federal authorities  

---

### Step 2: Contextual Research
- Investigated details surrounding Ulbricht’s arrest  
- Focused on:
  - law enforcement reports  
  - credible news articles  
  - official sources  

---

### Step 3: Source Analysis
- Located multiple articles describing the circumstances of the arrest  
- Identified references to:
  - the location of the arrest  
  - items confiscated by authorities  

---

### Step 4: Device Identification
- Found consistent reporting indicating the suspect was apprehended while actively using a laptop  
- Sources identified the device as a **Samsung laptop**

---

## Findings
- Suspect confirmed as Ross William Ulbricht  
- Arrest details verified through multiple sources  
- Confiscated device identified through credible reporting  
- Laptop brand consistently reported as **Samsung**

---

## Conclusion
Through identity verification and analysis of publicly available reporting, the confiscated laptop was determined to be a Samsung device. This conclusion was supported by multiple credible sources describing the suspect’s arrest and the equipment seized by authorities.

---

## Skills Demonstrated
- OSINT methodology  
- Identity verification  
- Contextual research  
- Source validation  
- Analytical reasoning  

---

## Lessons Learned
- Verifying whether a scenario is real or fictional is a critical first step  
- Official and reputable sources provide the most reliable investigative data  
- Contextual research can reveal key details not present in the initial scenario  
- Cross-referencing multiple sources increases confidence in findings  

---

## Final Determination
MAKE: SAMSUNG
